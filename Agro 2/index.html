Our Build

Yug-Krushi 2.0

An advanced, tractor-mounted automated transplanter engineered for precision planting in Indian vegetable farms. Designed for SAEINDIA TIFAN 2026, the system integrates 104-cavity tray handling, intelligent sapling detection, PTO-driven actuation, and synchronized planting mechanisms into a single field-ready prototype.

Flagship Project · 2026

SAEINDIA TIFAN 2026

Automated Multi-Vegetable Transplanter

🌾

Intelligent Tray Handling

Automated pickup mechanism designed for 104-cavity seedling trays. Ensures consistent sapling extraction and smooth conveyor-based feeding for uninterrupted transplanting.

🌿

Precision Planting System

Sensor-based sapling detection combined with synchronized furrow opening and plant push mechanism. Delivers uniform depth, spacing, and alignment for crops like chili, brinjal, and tomato.

⚙

PTO-Powered Drive & Battery

Tractor PTO integration powers the entire transplanting mechanism. Variable speed control enables adaptive planting based on field conditions and soil type.
